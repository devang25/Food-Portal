Excessive Food Listing Portal For Food Wastage.

The motive of our project is to develop a interface between the users who are looking for a channel to donate the excess food to needy instead of throwing it.


It enables us to donate the excess food by notifying users (Social Workers,and organizations who are registered with us) with the details of food that is available.

How It Works ?

. In our project the donor just have to scan the given QR code and fill up the form with the required information.


. The donor information is posted on our webpage and any number of user can claim the food.


. The system designed in the way that one or more user can claim the food , the registered users get the direct notification with the detail of the that is available in the form of mail.

Perks for Contributers !!

. Every time a person donates food, their picture along with amount of food donated will be displayed in Achievements section and on our social media pages.
